WOVO

Women Voice Organization

Overview:

Women Voice Organization (WOVO) was established to empower women and girls through skills training, advocacy, and community support initiatives. Since its founding in February 2025, the organization has successfully trained 51 women and girls in various livelihood and capacity-building programs.

Mission

Women Voice Organization (WOVO) exists to empower women and girls by equipping them with practical vocational skills, amplifying their voices within society, and fostering self-sustainability. Through training, mentorship, and advocacy, WOVO strives to create opportunities that enable women to achieve economic independence and become agents of change in their communities.
